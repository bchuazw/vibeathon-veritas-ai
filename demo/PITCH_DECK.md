# Veritas AI — One-Page Pitch Deck

> **The Future of Journalism, Automated**

---

## Problem

Breaking news takes **2-4 hours** to verify and publish. By then, social media has already spread unverified claims. Traditional newsrooms are too slow for the modern information landscape.

**Key pain points:**
- Manual fact-checking is slow and expensive
- Speed pressures lead to errors and retractions
- 24/7 coverage requires large editorial teams
- Readers can't verify source credibility

---

## Solution

**Veritas AI** is a fully autonomous, multi-agent newsroom that generates verified breaking news in **under 5 minutes**.

### Three Specialized AI Agents:

| Agent | Role | Output |
|-------|------|--------|
| 🕵️ **Scout** | Trend detection & source gathering | Verified source bundle |
| ✍️ **Content Creator** | Writing & fact-checking | Cited article draft |
| 📰 **Publisher** | Formatting & distribution | Published article |

**The Result:** Professional news articles with **mandatory source citations** and **confidence scores** — published faster than any human newsroom.

---

## Key Features

### ✅ Multi-Agent Fact-Checking
- Cross-references claims across multiple sources
- Calculates confidence scores algorithmically
- Flags contradictory information

### ✅ Source Transparency
- Every claim includes inline citations
- Sources section with clickable links
- Minimum 3 sources required

### ✅ Agent Visibility
- Real-time pipeline visualization
- See which agent is working and why
- Full audit trail of decisions

### ✅ Newspaper Aesthetic
- Clean, professional design
- Mobile-responsive layout
- Typography that evokes trust

### ✅ One-Click Generation
- No forms, no configuration
- Watch the pipeline in real-time
- Article delivered in minutes
