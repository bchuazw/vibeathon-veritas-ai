# Veritas AI — Demo Script (5 minutes)

> **Presenter:** Gladys  
> **Demo Link:** [https://veritas-ai.app](https://veritas-ai.app)  
> **Target:** Vibeathon Judges  

---

## Hook (30 sec)

**Opening line:**
> "What if AI could write breaking news in under 5 minutes?"

**Pause. Let that sink in.**

> "Traditional newsrooms take hours to verify, write, and publish breaking stories. By then, the news is old. Today, I'm going to show you how Veritas AI does it in under 5 minutes — with citations, fact-checking, and full transparency."

---

## Problem (45 sec)

**Set the scene:**

- **Newsrooms are slow** — Breaking news takes 2-4 hours to go from detection to publish
- **Fact-checking is manual** — Reporters manually verify sources, cross-reference claims
- **Error-prone** — Speed pressures lead to mistakes, retractions, credibility loss
- **24/7 coverage is expensive** — Requires large editorial teams across time zones

**Key stat to mention:**
> "The average breaking news story takes 3 hours to publish. In the social media age, that's a lifetime."

---

## Solution (1 min)

**Introduce Veritas AI:**

> "Veritas AI is a fully autonomous, multi-agent newsroom. Three specialized AI agents work together to detect, verify, and publish news — with no human intervention."

**The Three Agents:**

1. **🕵️ Scout Agent** — Monitors trends, detects breaking stories, gathers initial sources
2. **✍️ Content Creator Agent** — Writes the article, fact-checks claims, adds context
3. **📰 Publisher Agent** — Formats, adds metadata, publishes to the web

**The Promise:**
> "From detection to published article in under 5 minutes. Every source cited. Every claim verified."

---

## Live Demo (2 min)

### Step 1: Show Homepage (20 sec)
- Navigate to the Veritas AI homepage
- Point out the clean, newspaper-style aesthetic
- Show the "Generate Breaking News" button prominently displayed
- Mention: "This is what visitors see — a trusted news source"

### Step 2: Trigger Generation (15 sec)
- Click the "Generate Breaking News" button
- Explain: "This starts the pipeline — but in production, Scout would detect trends automatically"
- Show the loading state: "Article generation in progress..."

### Step 3: Watch Pipeline Run (30 sec)
- **Show the agent activity panel** — this is key for judges
- Narrate as each agent activates:
  - "Scout is gathering sources..."
  - "Content Creator is writing and fact-checking..."
  - "Publisher is formatting and publishing..."
- Point out real-time logs showing agent decisions

### Step 4: Show Published Article (30 sec)
- The article appears — scroll through it
- **Highlight:** Professional headline, clean layout, byline
- **Critical:** Point to the sources section — "Every claim is backed by a real source"
- Show 2-3 citations to prove source integration

### Step 5: Show Fact-Check Confidence (25 sec)
- Navigate to the fact-check panel
- Show the confidence score (e.g., "92% confidence")
- Explain: "This isn't just generated text — it's verified information"
- Show the breakdown: sources checked, claims verified, credibility ratings

**Narrative arc:** *Input → Pipeline → Output → Verification*

## Demo Tips for Presenter

### Do's ✅
- **Practice the timing** — 5 minutes is tight
- **Have a backup** — Screenshot the final article in case of network issues
- **Emphasize agent transparency** — Judges love seeing the "black box" opened
- **Point to citations** — This differentiates you from raw GPT output
- **Speak to the confidence score** — Shows sophistication in fact-checking

### Don'ts ❌
- Don't read slides — this is a demo, not a presentation
- Don't get bogged down in technical details — focus on the story
- Don't skip the source citations — they're your credibility anchor
- Don't rush the agent activity panel — judges want to see the process

### Backup Plan
If live demo fails:
1. Show pre-captured screenshot of the pipeline
2. Show pre-published article with sources
3. Explain what judges would have seen
